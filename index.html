Maxwell "Max" Raqueno

About Max

Maxwell "Max" Raqueno was born on January 2nd, 2015, and is a chihuahua-papillon mix. He lives in Southern Maryland with his adoptive family. Max loves belly rubs, nap time, and his family's shoes! He is very protective of those he loves, and is willing to bark whenever danger (or not) calls. Max is a loving pal, best friend, and a good boy.

Max's Family Members

Max is a part of the Raqueno family. His parents are Ron and Michelle Raqueno, and his sisters are Alyssa, Rachel, and Michaela Raqueno.

Max also has many members in his extended family! They include:

  • Auntie Lynette, and cousin Jazelle!
